Paneer Recipe

Description

Paneer Butter Masala is an Indian dish, very popular and tasty with gravy and people love it all over the world.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer chopped cubes
  • Paneer Butter Masala
  • Butter
  • Curry leaves
  • Red Chilly Powder
  • Tomatoes
  • Cream
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Cooking Oil

Steps:

  1. Chop Onions and tomatoes and grind them to make a purry
  2. Put oil in the frying pan and let it get warm, put masalas and tomatoes, onions, curry leaves and other stuff
  3. Add chopped paneer and stirr for 5 minutes
  4. Add cream
  5. Your Paneer Butter Masala is Ready
